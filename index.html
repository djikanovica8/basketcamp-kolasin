The Camp

Where the mountains make players.

For eleven summers, Kolašin has been the beating heart of youth basketball in the region. Founded by national-team coaches Vlado Šćepanović and Savo Đikanović, the camp blends NBA-level methodology with the magic of the mountains.

Two trainings a day, individual video analysis, psychology workshops and lectures, evening competitions and excursions into one of Europe's last untouched forests. Players leave better — and they leave with friends for life.

The Program

A day on the mountain.

  1. 07:30

    Morning training

    Fundamentals, footwork and shooting mechanics in age-based groups.

  2. 11:00

    Individual video analysis

    Every player is filmed and reviewed one-on-one — see yourself like the pros do.

  3. 13:00

    Workshops & lectures

    Sports psychology, nutrition and the mindset of a professional.

  4. 17:00

    Afternoon training

    Live play, scrimmages, 1-on-1, hot-shot and skill challenges.

  5. 20:30

    Evening competitions

    Tournaments, shooting contests and prizes — then campfire under the peaks.

  6. ∞

    Excursions & team life

    Trips into Biogradska Gora, pool time and friendships that last a lifetime.

Good to know

Frequently asked.

Who can attend?

Boys and girls roughly ages 8–16, from complete beginners to advanced players. Groups are selected by age and skill.

What's included in the price?

Two daily trainings, full board at Hotel Pini, video analysis, all competitions, workshops, excursions and supervision.

How do we reserve a spot?

Fill in the form below. A deposit of around €150 secures the place; the rest is settled before camp.

What should my child bring?

Basketball gear and sneakers, swimwear for the pool, and everyday clothes. We send a full checklist after registration.
