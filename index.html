The Camp

Where the mountains make players.

For twelve summers, Kolašin has been the beating heart of youth basketball in the region. Founded by Vlado Šćepanović and Savo Đikanović, the camp blends NBA-level methodology with the magic of the mountains.

Two trainings a day, psychology workshops and lectures, evening competitions and excursions into one of Europe's last untouched forests. Players leave better — and they leave with friends for life.

Good to know

Frequently asked.

What is Basketball Camp Kolašin?

The largest international youth basketball camp in Montenegro — for twelve summers it has brought kids from across the region and Europe to the mountain town of Kolašin. Founded by Vlado Šćepanović and Savo Đikanović with a single goal: through top-level training, competition and mountain life, every child leaves better — as a player and as a person.

Who is the camp for?

Boys and girls aged 8 to 16 — from complete beginners to experienced players. Groups are formed by age and skill level, so every child trains in the company that pushes them forward fastest.

When does the camp take place?

Summer 2027 brings three sessions: Session I 27 Jun – 2 Jul, Session II 2 – 7 Jul, and Session III — Skills & Shooting Camp 7 – 12 Jul, a dedicated program focused on shooting.

What does the program include?

Two trainings a day, evening 5-on-5 games and competitions, individual work with the coaches, sports-psychology workshops and talks, lectures, excursions and free time — plus challenges like 1-on-1, hot-shot and skills contests.

What's included in the price?

All of it — two daily trainings, full board at Hotel Pini Apart (three meals a day), every competition, workshop and excursion, and round-the-clock supervision by the coaching team.

Where do the kids stay?

At Hotel Pini Apart — apartments with full board, minutes from the courts. The coaches stay in the same hotel and are with the kids around the clock, with the pool and hotel facilities on hand.

How do I sign up?

Fastest — through the registration page. You can also write to us at basketballcampkolasin@gmail.com, call 067 032 132, or message us on WhatsApp via the green icon in the corner.

Do I need to reserve a spot in advance?

Yes — a deposit of around €150 secures your child's place, and the rest is settled before camp starts. Spots fill up fast every year, so don't leave it for the last minute.

What should my child pack?

Basketball gear and shoes for indoor and outdoor courts, swimwear for the pool, something warm for mountain evenings and everyday clothes. Every camper receives a full checklist after registration.
